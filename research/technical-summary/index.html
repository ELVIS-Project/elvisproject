Technical Summary

A relatively brief overview of many technical decisions made during the ELVIS Project's grant period. This presentation is based on one given by Christopher Antila at the final formal ELVIS meeting on 16 December 2013.

The presentation works in your browser. When you view the presentation, use the 'p' button to show or hide the presenter's notes. Use the left and right arrow keys to change slides Click here to view the presentation in a new tab or window.

Since the presentation is an HTML file (made with remark.js) you can easily download and save its source, then find the associated JavaScript and SVG files. Of course, you may also download the LilyPond source files for the examples as a zip file or a much smaller XZ-compressed tarball.

If you have questions or comments, please contact Christopher Antila (christopher@antila.ca).
